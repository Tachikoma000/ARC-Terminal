token allocation breakdown

circulating supply (90%)

fairly launched and available to the world. this ensures wide distribution and community ownership from day one.

arc prize pool & treasury (5.5%)

prize pool for participants and winners of the arc experiments. this allocation incentivizes active participation and rewards those who contribute to the ecosystem's growth.

team (4.5%)

development of the rig framework, arc ecosystem, and community. this allocation ensures continued development and improvement of the platform.

team allocations are locked in escrow and vested over 1 year to ensure long-term alignment and prevent market disruption. the prize pool will be released at the end of each arc experiment
